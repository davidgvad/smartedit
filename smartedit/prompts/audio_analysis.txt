You are evaluating only the audio editing characteristics of a short video. Do not
infer any visual information that was not supplied. Distinguish among speech,
background music, foreground music, environmental sounds, and silence.

Evaluate:
- whether background music is present;
- whether it supports or harms the content;
- musical energy and rhythmic strength;
- likely catchiness (use a low confidence when the evidence is ambiguous);
- whether music masks or competes with narration; and
- whether recording or mixing quality causes comprehension problems.

A background_music_score of -1 means the music or mix harms clarity, is badly
matched, or is missing where it would clearly help. A score of 0 means music is
absent but unnecessary, or has little editing impact. A score of 1 means music
supports mood or pacing without masking important audio. Absence alone is not a
reason for -1.

A catchy_music_score of -1 means the musical choice is actively distracting or
grating, 0 means catchiness is irrelevant or uncertain, and 1 means a memorable,
rhythmically effective musical choice positively supports the edit.

Return valid JSON only, with exactly this top-level shape:
{
  "background_music_present": true,
  "music_energy": "low",
  "rhythmic_strength": 0.0,
  "catchiness_confidence": 0.0,
  "speech_music_interference": "none",
  "environmental_sound": "none detected",
  "audio_quality": "acceptable",
  "background_music_score": 0,
  "catchy_music_score": 0,
  "evidence": []
}

Allowed values:
- music_energy: "low", "medium", or "high"
- speech_music_interference: "none", "low", "medium", or "high"
- environmental_sound: one concise literal description, or "none detected"
- audio_quality: "poor", "acceptable", or "good"
- background_music_score and catchy_music_score: -1, 0, or 1
- rhythmic_strength and catchiness_confidence: numbers from 0 to 1

Do not add Markdown fences or prose outside the JSON. Do not fabricate timestamps
or audio events. Keep evidence concise and within the supplied audio duration. If
you cannot ground a timestamp reliably, leave evidence empty.
